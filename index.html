How to Use
Drawing Cards

Tap a card to reveal its face. Tap again to see the card symbol enlarged. Long-press to flip it back.

Question Input

If you see an example question in the input field, tap it to use that question as-is.

Decks
  • Universal Waite — The classic 78-card tarot deck (22 Major + 56 Minor Arcana). Supports upright and reversed readings.
  • Major Arcana — A sub-deck of just the 22 Major cards from Universal Waite. Best for exploring life's bigger themes and turning points.
  • Lenormand — A 36-card oracle deck focused on practical, everyday situations and concrete context.
  • Oracle Belline — A 52-card French oracle deck used for reading the flow of time and signs of what's ahead.

These are general tendencies — you're free to use any deck for any question.

Upright & Reversed

Universal Waite and Major Arcana support upright and reversed orientations. Use the toggle below the tabs to enable or disable this feature.

Bottom Buttons
  • 🔀 Shuffle — Reshuffle the deck.
  • ✨ View Spread — See the cards you've drawn, organized by deck. You can copy the result.
  • ✶ Tarot Reading — AI reads your cards using carefully crafted interpretation guidelines. Receive an objective and insightful reading drawn from the cards' symbols and spread.
  • 📜 Journal — View your past readings.
Spread Guide

Start by drawing 1 or 3 cards. Whatever number feels right, your reading will follow your question.

Advanced Guide
  • 1 card — Today's Card: The core message of your current situation.
  • 2 cards — Two Paths: Situation / Guidance.
  • 3 cards — Three-Card: [Past-Present-Future] or [Situation-Action-Outcome] depending on the question.
    You can also request your preferred interpretation directly in the question field.
    Example: "Read it as past-present-future"
  • 5 cards — Star Spread: Center, top, bottom, left, right — a panoramic view.
  • 7 cards — Horseshoe: A 7-step arc from past to future.
  • 10 cards — Celtic Cross: The classic 10-position deep reading.
  • Lenormand · Oracle Belline: Used alongside Waite cards to add practical context and timing.
Drawing from multiple decks
  • Draw Universal Waite 3 + Lenormand 2 + Oracle Belline 2 (great for a focused question), or 4 + 3 + 3 (great for a deeper question), and the three decks are automatically read as one combined story — no extra instructions needed.
Example — drawing 4-3-3
  • Question: "How will this relationship unfold?"
    → All 10 cards across the three decks are read as one combined story.
  • Question: "How will this relationship unfold? no combo"
    → The 4 Waite cards are read as the main spread, while the 3 Lenormand and 3 Belline cards are added only as brief supplemental notes.
    (Adding "no combo" to your question turns off the combined reading.)
Want a different layout?
  • If you'd like a layout other than these, just describe it in the question field.
    Example: "I drew 5 cards. Read them as five stages of a relationship (beginning–conflict–turning point–recovery–future)"

Journal Storage

Readings are automatically saved when you tap View Spread or Tarot Reading. Data is stored locally on this device only and is not synced across devices. You can back up your readings using Export / Import in the Journal screen.

Focus Mode

Tap an empty area on the card screen to hide the header and buttons. Tap again to bring them back.
